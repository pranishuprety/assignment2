The Club Information of Real Madrid

Achievements

Real Madrid has won many prestigious titles, including:

  • UEFA Champions League (15 times)
  • La Liga (36 times)
  • Copa del Rey (20 times)
  • FIFA Club World Cup (5 times)

Real Madrid, founded in 1902, is one of the most successful football clubs in the world. Situated in Madrid, Spain, they are known for their iconic white kits and play at the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium. The club has a rich history of legendary players and memorable victories. Their passionate fan base spans the globe, including in our very own Caldwell University.
